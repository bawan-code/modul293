The Cosmere

The Author

Brandon Sanderson is a prolific American fantasy and science fiction author, best known for his intricate magic systems, expansive worldbuilding, and methodical storytelling. Born in 1975 in Nebraska, he gained widespread recognition with his Mistborn series and for completing Robert Jordan’s The Wheel of Time after Jordan’s death. He is also the creator of The Cosmere, a vast shared universe that connects many of his novels through a hidden overarching narrative.

The Story

The Cosmere spans multiple series and stand-alone books, including Mistborn, The Stormlight Archive, Elantris, and Warbreaker. Each takes place on a different planet with its own unique magic system, but they are all subtly linked by a deeper story involving god-like entities called Shards of Adonalsium, recurring characters like the mysterious Hoid, and the ability of some characters to travel between worlds. Thematically, the books explore questions of divinity, power, identity, and sacrifice, all while delivering compelling plots and rich character arcs. Sanderson’s ability to weave these elements into a cohesive, interconnected universe has made him a defining voice in modern fantasy.
